Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how WileyWorks LLC collects and uses information when you use our products and services.

Information we may collect

  • Information you provide, such as account details or support messages.
  • Usage and device information, such as app activity, device identifiers, and approximate diagnostics, used to keep the service working and secure.
  • Safety and moderation information, such as reports, blocks, and related metadata used to enforce rules and prevent abuse.

How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To keep users safe, prevent fraud, and enforce rules.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.

How information may be shared

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We may share information with service providers that help us operate the service, under confidentiality obligations.
  • We may share information if required by law, or to protect users, our services, and our rights.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the service, resolve issues, enforce rules, and comply with legal obligations. Safety related records may be retained longer to prevent repeat abuse.

Your choices

  • You can request account deletion by emailing us.
  • You can report abusive content or users through in app reporting tools where available.
